Canada Post Transformation Project

Client-focused integration work delivered during your Accenture experience.

Project Overview

Worked on a Canada Post transformation initiative focused on improving enterprise integrations and supporting reliable data flow between SAP ERP and external logistics or business-facing systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Designed and supported SAP CPI integration flows for communication between SAP ERP and external applications.
  • Configured enterprise adapters such as HTTP, SOAP, OData, IDoc, and SFTP based on interface needs.
  • Developed message mappings and used Groovy scripting for payload transformation and custom processing.
  • Worked with XML and JSON structures to support reliable message exchange.
  • Monitored production interfaces, analyzed failures, and resolved issues to maintain system availability.

Impact

  • Supported modernization of legacy integration processes into more scalable cloud-based interfaces.
  • Improved deployment stability through testing support, monitoring, and defect resolution.
  • Contributed to business continuity by ensuring production-ready integrations and faster issue turnaround.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams during requirement analysis, deployment, and incident handling.

Technologies Used

SAP CPI, SAP ERP, SAP BTP, Groovy, XML, JSON, HTTP, SOAP, OData, IDoc, SFTP, monitoring & incident support
