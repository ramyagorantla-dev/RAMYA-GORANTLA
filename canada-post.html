Canada Post Transformation Project

Client project focused on development, implementation, and enterprise integration delivery.

Project Overview

Worked on a Canada Post transformation initiative centered on designing and developing enterprise integration solutions to improve communication between SAP systems and external business applications.

My Role

Contributed as a developer by building integration flows, implementing technical solutions, creating mappings, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to support business requirements and delivery goals.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and developed SAP CPI integration flows for enterprise communication between SAP and external systems.
  • Implemented adapters such as HTTP, SOAP, OData, IDoc, and SFTP based on interface requirements.
  • Developed mappings and transformation logic using XML, JSON, and Groovy scripting.
  • Worked with cross-functional teams including analysts, testers, and business stakeholders to deliver technical solutions.
  • Contributed to interface design, implementation, and deployment-ready development activities.

  • SAP BTP
  • Groovy
